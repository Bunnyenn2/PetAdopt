About us

Get to know the warriors behind DCR! Learn why they are so passionate about working diligently!

Hello, My name is Scarlett Spicedust, and I'm the founder of DCR. When I got my first Slime at a young age, I was sincerely overjoyed, I love taking my slime on trips and playing with her. When I learned that plenty of creatures were not treated as well as mine, I vowed to help those in need to live full, healthy and happy lives.

Hi, My name is Cirrus, and I'm the Co-founder at DCR. Ever since I was a little fae, I've been really lucky to be surrounded by those who really shown me the wonders of dungeon creatures. Even now, as I grew up I fell more in love in what they can really offer us warriors. I want to give back and show them the joy of having a best friend.

I don't like to show my face. I'm the Financial accountant here at DCR. I think everyone deserves a good home and I'm working my hardest to provide that here in DCR. Thanks.
